## Intro

Frappe is a _full stack_, _batteries-included_, web framework written in Python and Javascript

Frappe is primarily powers ERPNext.

ERPNext is a free, open-source, comprehensive business management software (ERP).

ERPNext is amazing because its pretty much an end to end solution for Business Management for most use cases.
